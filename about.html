About Us

Welcome to Daivik Bharat, the land of the Gods.

From the highest peaks of the Himalayas to the deepest trenches of the Indian Ocean, from the Rann of Kutch to the tea farms of Darjeeling, from the Phulkari of Punjab to the Kanjeevaram of Tamil Nadu, from chole bhagore to rava dose, what is astonishing about Bharat is not it’s diversity, but the unity and strength it continues to show even with such vast differences. Standing the test of time, various invasions, multiple attacks and a plethora of propagandas internally, as well as externally, we seem to be losing the right perception through which we can truly appreciate our Maa.

The idea of compiling together different information that we find here and there started as a small textual conversation on random evening between two college students who wanted to bring together people that share the same admiration for their motherland as they do. The result of that you see today as Daivik Bharat. Our aim is to bring to light what is hidden in shadows and to recall what is forgotten.

Join us as we embark on this journey of rediscovering ourselves as we delve into discovery the hidden parts of our great nation.

Avinash Pathy and Srishti Tyagi are both final year BE students, with a keen interest in Indian history, Politics and Sanatan Dharma. This common interest lead them to build this site to share the information they acquire, as well as create Instagram and Twitter pages for exchange of views.
