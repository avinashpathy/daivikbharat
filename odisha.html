Famous temples in Odisha

There are more than 700 temples in Odisha. Some famous temples in Odisha are :

Jagannath Temple, Puri
Sun Temple, Konark
Lingaraja Temple, Bhubaneswar
Maa Tara Tarini Temple, Purusottampur
Gundicha Temple, Puri
Nilamadhaba Temple, Kantilo
Brahmeswara Temple, Bhubaneswar
Parsurameswara Temple, Bhubaneswar
Maa Cuttack Chandi Temple, Cuttack
